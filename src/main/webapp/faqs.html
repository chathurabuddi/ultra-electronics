  • HOW CAN I PAY FOR AN ONLINE ORDER ?
    • A payment method can be chosen from the given options while making an online order in the “Review & Payments” stage.
      The options available are:
      Bank Transfer / Bank Deposit - Bank account details will be given when this option is selected. You have to continue with the order and finalize it by placing the order by clicking on the “Place Order” button. You can then make the payment by transferring money to our bank account. The order will be shipped only after a payment is made.
      Cheque / Money order - The required details for this will be displayed when this option is selected. The order will be shipped only after the cheque or the money order has arrived.
      Dialog eZ Cash - An eZ Cash guide on how to make the payment will be provided when selecting this option.
      Credit Card / PayPal (2Checkout) - When this option is selected continue to the final “Order Review” stage and click on the “Place Order” button. Then you will be automatically redirected to a website (www.2checkout.com). Here you make the payment either directly through a credit/ debit card or through your paypal account. Once you submit the payment you will be redirected back to the Lankatronics website and you will see the 9 digit order number.
